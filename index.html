Ling - Skills & Description

Description: Ling is a high-mobility assassin who can traverse walls and eliminate targets swiftly.

  • Passive: Cloud Walker - Gains extra crit chance and energy regen on walls.
  • Skill 1: Finch Poise - Climbs walls to reposition quickly.
  • Skill 2: Defiant Sword - Dashes and deals crit damage.
  • Ultimate: Tempest of Blades - AoE slash, slows, and resets cooldowns.
Lancelot - Skills & Description

Description: Lancelot is an assassin known for burst damage and graceful dashes.

  • Passive: Soul Cutter - Increases damage dealt to enemies with his skills.
  • Skill 1: Puncture - Dash through enemies, marking them.
  • Skill 2: Thorned Rose - AoE burst in a triangle area.
  • Ultimate: Phantom Execution - Charge and slash enemies in a line.
Esmeralda - Skills & Description

Description: Esmeralda is a tank/mage who absorbs enemy shields while sustaining damage.

  • Passive: Starmoon Casket - Converts enemy shields into her own HP.
  • Skill 1: Frostmoon Shield - Gains shield and movement speed.
  • Skill 2: Stardust Dance - AoE damage with each swing.
  • Ultimate: Falling Starmoon - Jumps and slams for massive AoE damage.

About Us

"Victory is not just about strength, but about strategy, teamwork, and passion."

Our History

Founded in 2020, MLNation began as a small community of passionate Mobile Legends players. Over the years, we've grown into a trusted platform for players of all levels, offering resources, strategies, and guides to improve gameplay. Our commitment to supporting the community has helped us become a go-to hub for Mobile Legends enthusiasts worldwide.

Core Values

  • Community - We believe in the power of community and the importance of collaboration and support among players.
  • Integrity - We maintain honesty and fairness in all our interactions and content.
  • Excellence - We strive to provide the best resources, guides, and strategies for our users to help them achieve their full potential.
  • Innovation - We're always adapting and evolving with the game, ensuring we stay ahead with the latest strategies and trends.

Our Philosophy

At MLNation, we believe that success in Mobile Legends is not solely about individual skill, but about learning, adapting, and growing as a team. Our approach combines practical strategies with mental and emotional preparation to empower players to think strategically, communicate effectively, and play with passion.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower Mobile Legends players by providing them with updated strategies, comprehensive guides, and a supportive community. We aim to help players level up their game, from beginners to pros, and create an inclusive environment where every player can thrive.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every Mobile Legends player has access to the resources, guidance, and support they need to improve and enjoy the game to its fullest. We strive to be the ultimate destination for all things Mobile Legends, uniting players with knowledge, tools, and a sense of belonging.
